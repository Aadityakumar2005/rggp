PRINCIPAL MESSAGE

It's an immense pleasure to welcome you all to Rajiv Gandhi Govt. Polytechnic, Narwana. We aim to provide quality technical educationwith great emphasis on human values, technical, professional, entrepreneurial skills for sustainable growth of society and nation as a whole. We believe that the purpose of education is to facillitate the development of clarity of aspirations and adequate competence to actualize it.

Since its establishment in 2009, the institute has made its presence strongly felt in the technical education community not only in the region but at state level too. A range of achievement in academics in all six branches is attained by joint efforts of all stake holders. Our very young alumni continue to keep the institute name high.


This institute is well equipped with laboratory, workshop, rich collectionof book/resources in library, computing facility, internet facilityand industry-institute interaction facilities which enriches the process of learning. Our teaching learning process is based on practical, inclusive and motivational approach. In addition to excellent teaching, the faculty members are actively involved in over all training and placement activities of the students and monitoring them about their carrier path and overall development. Our faculty is also actively attending & participating seminar/workshops/training & conferencing conducted by NITTTR/AICTE and other eminent Institute time to time enrich their knowledge and to update with latest technology. With the guidance of seniors, supports from colleagues and decoration of diligent workforce, I wish and hope this premier institute to scale higher heights.

Thank you
With warm regard
Mr. Jaswant Singh
Principal
